About Me

Hi, I'm Marcos — a backend developer who genuinely loves this craft. I work mostly with Python, building robust, scalable REST APIs with Django, Django REST Framework, and FastAPI.

Day to day I reach for PostgreSQL and MySQL, Celery and Redis for asynchronous processing and caching, and Docker, AWS, and GitHub Actions to ship and run things in production. I also enjoy working with integrations and authentication (OAuth2, JWT), and lately I've been diving into production AI: LLM-powered features and RAG built on top of the stack I already know well.

More than any single tool, I love learning and getting a little better every day. I like treating performance as engineering, keeping code clean and simple, and understanding why things work the way they do.

This blog is where I share what I learn along the way. Feedback is always welcome — if you have thoughts about a post or just want to chat, reach out on the networks below.
